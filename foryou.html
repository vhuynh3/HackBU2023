The Humane Society!
167 Conklin Ave, Binghamton, NY 13903
12:00pm - 05:00pm
Sign Up




The Humane Society of Broome County is a non-profit animal welfare organization dedicated to providing care and finding homes for animals in need. Their mission is to promote the humane treatment of animals, prevent animal cruelty and neglect, and reduce pet overpopulation through spay and neuter programs.
The organization operates an animal shelter and also have programs in place to support thadoption of pets, including low-cost spay and neuter services and pet adoption events.
To support the work of the Humane Society of Broome County, you can make a donation, volunteer your time, or consider adopting a pet from their shelter.



Animal Care Council!
131 Washington Ave, Endicott, NY 13760
3:30pm - 06:30pm
Sign Up




The Animal Care Council (ACC) is a non-profit organization that provides support and resources to animal welfare organizations and agencies. Their mission is to improve the lives of animals through education, advocacy, and collaboration.
The ACC works to provide resources and support to animal welfare organizations and agencies, including shelters, rescues, and spay and neuter programs. They offer training and education to staff and volunteers, promote animal welfare initiatives and policies, and provide funding and grants to organizations working to improve the lives of animals.
If you're interested in supporting the work of the Animal Care Council, you can volunteer!
